REELSLIDER

Real video controls for Instagram. Seek, volume & playback speed on Reels, Stories, Feed & Posts.

Installed & Active — v1.0.0
What You Get
⏩
Seek & Scrub
Click anywhere on the timeline to jump. Drag to scrub through any video.
🔊
Volume Control
Set default volume from the popup. Your choice persists across sessions.
⚡
Playback Speed
Speed up or slow down. 0.25× to 2.0× — remembered across all videos.
🔇
Mute Memory
Your mute preference sticks while scrolling. No more re-unmuting every reel.
⌨️
Keyboard Shortcuts
Navigate hands-free with A/S/D for seek, M for mute, F for fullscreen.
🔁
Loop Sections
Set loop points with [ and ] keys. Perfect for learning choreo or music.
📺
Works Everywhere
Reels, Stories, Feed videos, Posts, Profile reels tab — native controls on every Instagram video.

Getting Started
1
Open Instagram — navigate to any Reel, Story, or Feed video. Controls appear automatically.
2
Click the extension icon in your toolbar to adjust speed, volume, and mute preferences.
3
Use keyboard shortcuts for hands-free control while scrolling through Reels.
Privacy & Trust

ReelSlider is free & open source. It runs entirely in your browser — no data leaves your device. No accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, no subscriptions.

All preferences are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server, ever.
